Steps
  • Oven: Set oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a 9x13 inch pan.
  • Mix Dry Ingredients: Combine 1 cup flour and 1 ½ tsp baking powder in a bowl.
  • Cream Butter and Sugar: In another bowl, beat ½ cup softened butter with 1 cup sugar until fluffy.
  • Add Eggs and Vanilla: Mix in 5 eggs, one at a time, and stir in 1 tsp vanilla extract.
  • Combine Mixtures: Gradually add the flour mixture and ½ cup milk to the wet ingredients until well combined.
  • Bake: Pour batter into the pan and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out clean. Let it cool.
  • Prepare Milk Mixture: In a bowl, whisk together 1 can (12 oz) evaporated milk, 1 can (14 oz) sweetened condensed milk, and 1 cup whole milk or cream.
  • Soak Cake: Poke holes in the cooled cake and pour the milk mixture over it. Refrigerate for 4 hours or overnight.
  • Make Whipped Cream: Whip 1 cup heavy cream with 2 tbsp powdered sugar and 1 tsp vanilla until soft peaks form.
  • Serve: Spread whipped cream on the cake, garnish with fruit if desired, and enjoy!
